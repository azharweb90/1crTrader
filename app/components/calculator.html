This is your main screen — log today's trade outcomes here to stay inside your daily risk limits. Completed days automatically update your running balance shown in the header and roadmap.

Points SL = today's max rupee loss ÷ (lot quantity × lots). Edit lots directly above for today's trade size; add or remove instruments via "Change Tier." Enter your own real stop-loss in points to see what it would actually cost in rupees at your current lot count — independent of the flat Points SL figure, which always equals today's max loss exactly. If that figure exceeds what your tier allows for the lots you're using, it's flagged in red — shown for your own calculation, never blocked.

Max Lots is a ceiling, not a requirement — you can always trade fewer. Max Loss does NOT multiply by lots: whether you use 1 lot or your full allowance, your loss limit for the day stays the same. Lots increase either by starting with more capital (you enter directly at the matching row) or by growing your balance 50% above where you started (this only ever moves you up, never back down on a losing day). Maximum 2 trades per day, with a 30-minute cooldown between them.
