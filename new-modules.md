Key Modules for Your Trading ToolPosition Size Calculator (Topic 5)Purpose: Prevents traders from over-leveraging or risking too much capital on a single trade.Inputs: Account Equity ($), Risk Percentage per Trade (%), Entry Price ($), Stop Loss Price ($).Outputs: Total Position Size (Units/Shares/Lots) and Max Dollar Risk ($).Trade Expectancy Calculator (Topics 1, 2, & 11)Purpose: Shows whether a strategy is mathematically profitable long-term.Inputs: Win Rate (%), Average Win (\(or R), Average Loss (\) or R).Outputs: Expected Value per Trade ($ or R) and Minimum Required Win Rate to Break Even.Drawdown & Recovery Matrix (Topics 6 & 7)Purpose: Educates traders on asymmetric drawdown risk and losing streaks.Inputs: Account Loss Percentage (%) or Max Expected Consecutive Losses.Outputs: Gain Required to Get Back to Even (%) and Projected Drawdown ($).Equity Growth Simulator (Topics 3, 8, & 10)Purpose: Projects potential account trajectories based on trade frequency and compound risk settings.Inputs: Starting Capital ($), Expected Trades per Month, Win Rate (%), Risk per Trade (%).Outputs: 6-Month or 1-Year Projected Equity Curve.
