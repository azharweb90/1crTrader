The Rules Engine

Four mechanics, doing the holding-back for you.

None of this is advice on what to trade. All of it is about making sure you survive long enough for your edge to play out.

01

A daily loss limit you can't argue with

Set once from your tier and current balance — a flat ₹1,800 for Small accounts, 2% of capital from Medium upward. Hit 75% of it and Trade 2 is soft-blocked; hit 100% and the day is over, no exceptions.

02

A forced cooldown, not a suggestion

Close Trade 1 in profit and you're asked to choose: bank the win and stop, or take a real 30-minute break before Trade 2 unlocks. The break is enforced by a countdown, not your willpower.

03

Your broker's data, honestly reported

Connect Zerodha, Angel One, or Upstox and your trades import automatically — including the ones that broke your own rules. Nothing gets hidden to make your week look better than it was.

04

A journal that grades discipline, not just P&L

Every trade gets a weighted review score across setup, entry, management, and reflection — plus a weekly breakdown of exactly which rule you broke and how many times.
